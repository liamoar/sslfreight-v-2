Sea Freight

Sea Freight, also known as ocean freight, is one of the longest established forms of transporting goods internationally. It’s also one of the most economical. We deliver highly flexible services through partnerships with leading ocean carriers from all the ports. Our sea freight services include:
1.FCL(Full Container Load)
2.Refer & Normal container,
3.LCL(Less Than Container Load),
4.Non-Containerised load & break bulk cargos.

InLand Freight

Inland Haulage mode is by road or rail, it cover transportation from suppliers or consignees to the port of shipment and from discharge port to the point of stripping the ocean container by truck and or rail.
We combine simple, standardized Road & Rail Freight products with a host of customizable options to achieve the ideal balance of lead-time, capacity, frequency and cost. SSL’ freight specialists know the key to a successful supply chain is a committed relationship with our customer.
